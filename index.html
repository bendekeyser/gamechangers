About

What

Gamechangers is a project started by 2 crypto enthusiasts that are very interested in the NFT part of it. It’s an exclusive nft collectible project created to honor the world’s most important people that made an impact on how we live today.

How

The collection consists of 26 artworks of 26 famous, important people. The names of these people are not know but you will recognize the most.
For the people who don’t recognize the person, we implemented a hidden tip in the artwork that has a link with the person on it.

When

Every 2 weeks, we will launch 1 of the 26 artworks until we’ve launched them all (launch dates are mentioned below).

Where

Every artwork will become available on Rarible for 0.1 ETH. You can find these by searching 'gamechangers' on Rarible or by clicking this link.
